Professional experiance

I have a diverse work background that includes experience in audit analysis, delivery management, and lumber mill operations. Along with my skills in coding and web design, I also possess specialized skills in operating heavy machinery, specifically forklifts. As an Audit Analyst, I have gained valuable experience in auditing invoices, analyzing data, and identifying discrepancies. My attention to detail and analytical skills makes me an asset in ensuring accuracy and compliance in proper billing. In my previous role as a Delivery Manager, I demonstrated excellent organizational and leadership skills, managing orders and coordinating schedules to ensure timely and efficient delivery of goods. This requires a high level of coordination and communication to ensure successful deliveries, which is a testament to my ability to work collaboratively and handle complex logistics. My experience in the lumber mill industry has given me a deep understanding of production processes and safety regulations. I have operated heavy machinery, specifically forklifts, which requires attention to detail and excellent hand-eye coordination. Overall, I have a wide range of skills and experience that can be valuable in a variety of industries. Ihave demonstrated adaptability and a willingness to take on new challenges. With my diverse background, I am well-positioned to make valuable contributions to any team or organization I work with. With a diverse skill set and a passion for creating meaningful change, I am poised to make a lasting impact in the field of software engineering and beyond. This experience has given me a unique perspective on the challenges faced by people in different parts of the world and a drive to make a positive impact. My Values

I hold the value of family at the center of my life. I believe that strong families form the foundation of strong communities, and I prioritize spending quality time with my loved ones. I am committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for my family and am always there to support them in their endeavors. As a religious individual, I am a proud member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. My faith has taught me to value compassion, service, and kindness. I believe that we are all children of God, and that we should treat others with respect and love, regardless of their beliefs or backgrounds. I am a hard worker who believes in the value of putting in effort and dedication to achieve success. I take pride in my work and strive to consistently improve and grow in my professional endeavors. Honesty is a core value that I hold dear. I believe in always telling the truth, even when it is difficult or uncomfortable. I also believe in being accountable for my actions and taking responsibility for my mistakes. Loyalty is another value that is important to me. I am committed to being there for my family, friends, and colleagues through thick and thin. I am a faithful and dependable person who can be counted on to be there when needed. As a leader, I believe in leading by example and empowering those around me to reach their full potential. I strive to listen to the needs of others, communicate effectively, and work collaboratively to achieve common goals.
