Grand canyon three-night trip:

Experience the thrill of rafting through the towering walls of the Grand Canyon on a 3-day adventure. With expert guides, you'll navigate the powerful rapids of the Colorado River and take in stunning views of one of the world's most iconic natural wonders. You'll have the opportunity to explore side canyons, hike to waterfalls, and camp under the stars. This trip is perfect for those who want a taste of Grand Canyon rafting without committing to a longer trip.

Cost: $1475

Dates: April 30 May 13, 20, 27, 30 June 3, 10, 17, 24, 28 July 1, 8, 15, 22, 29 August 5, 12, 19, 26 September 9, 17

Grand Canyon six-night trip:

Embark on an epic 6-day journey through the heart of the Grand Canyon. This trip offers a more immersive experience, allowing you to truly soak in the grandeur of this iconic landscape. You'll navigate rapids ranging from class II to class V, explore hidden waterfalls and pools, and hike through narrow slot canyons. Your expert guides will share their knowledge of the geology, history, and ecology of the canyon, providing a truly educational experience. This trip is perfect for those who want to fully immerse themselves in the Grand Canyon.

Cost: $2250

Dates: April 25 May 8, 15, 22, 25, 29 June 5, 12, 19, 23, 26 July 3, 10, 17, 24, 31 August 7, 14, 21 September 4, 12

Salmon River five-night trip:

Join us for an unforgettable 5-day rafting trip down the Salmon River, also known as the "River of No Return." This trip offers a unique blend of excitement and relaxation as you navigate through challenging rapids and serene stretches of calm water. Along the way, you'll have the opportunity to fish for trout, hike to scenic overlooks, and soak in natural hot springs. Our experienced guides will provide insight into the natural and cultural history of the area, making this trip an educational and enriching experience.

Cost: $1540

Dates: June 3, 10, 17, 24 July 2

Desolation six-night trip

Discover the rugged beauty of Utah's Desolation Canyon on a 6-day rafting adventure. This trip offers a remote wilderness experience, with towering canyon walls and abundant wildlife. You'll navigate through rapids ranging from class II to class IV, hike to ancient Native American ruins, and camp under the stars in beautiful riverside campsites. Our expert guides will share their knowledge of the area's geology, ecology, and history, making this trip an enriching and educational experience. This trip is perfect for those who want to escape the crowds and immerse themselves in the natural beauty of Utah's backcountry.

Cost: $1790

Dates: July 10, 18, 26 Aug 3, 11, 19, 27 Sept 4
